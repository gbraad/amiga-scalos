Authors

David McMinn dave@scalos.co.ukPlugins, Libraries, Assembler to C conversion
Jürgen Lachmann jurgen@scalos.co.ukPlugins, Datatypes, Assembler to C conversion

You may contact all the development team generally using the info@scalos.co.uk email address.
