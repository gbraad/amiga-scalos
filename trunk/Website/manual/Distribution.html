Distribution

The Scalos archive may be copied and distributed on the condition that it is left complete and unmodified in all its parts. If you have any questions regarding distribution in a certain manner please consult the development team before going ahead.

You may charge only a nominal fee for the re-distribution of Scalos (equivalent to the cost of a floppy disk) without the permission of the authors

Inclusion on magazine cover CDs is allowed, as long as the authors are notified before the CD goes to duplication. However distribution of Scalos and its associated extras (modules, plugins, website contents) on a packaged CD-ROM or set of floppy disks is not allowed!
