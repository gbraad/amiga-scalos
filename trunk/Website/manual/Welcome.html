Welcome

The Amiga line of personal computers offers a unique combination of versatility and usability.

This guide introduces you to our Scalos desktop replacement for the Amiga computer. Using Scalos you'll be able get things done easier and faster using the GUI to control ther Amiga using a mouse to select graphic symbols in the form of icons, or small pictures, and list-like menus. The Scalos desktop is easy to learn because these icons and menus are displayed automatically. You do not have to memorize a long list of commands to accomplish a task.

If you work your way through this guide it will show you how to do the most common tasks, some advice on doing things better and points out some of the exciting new features Scalos brings to your desktop compared to the old Amiga Workbench.
