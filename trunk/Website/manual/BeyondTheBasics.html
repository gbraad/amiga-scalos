Whats On Your Computer

The desktop, Scalos, is your first way to access your files easily. Using your mouse cursor you can clcik your way through the directory structure of your hard disk drive, floppy drive, CD-ROM, ZIP disk - what ever device is installed on your Amiga.

To See Whats On Your Device

The desktop contains icons for the various devices which contain media at this present moment.
