Help On A Specific Item

By placing your mouse cursor over a certain GUI component and leaving it still for a user defined number of seconds you will be presented with a help balloon containing some useful information on exactly what it does. Move your mouse cursor and it will dissapear again. [window grab here]

On-Line Help

If at any time you wish to get some help or advice on doing a certain task you can consult the help guide by selecting the "Help..." option from the "About" pull-down menu. This can be located by holding the right mouse button down and moving your mouse cursor to the top of the screen over the 'About' option in the menu bar. [screen menu bar grab]
